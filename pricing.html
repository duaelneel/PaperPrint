Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our pricing and packages.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for larger orders. The discount percentage varies by product type and quantity. For substantial orders or recurring business, we can create custom pricing packages. Contact our sales team to discuss volume pricing options for your specific needs.

Can I customize the packages to fit my needs?

Absolutely! Our packages are designed to provide a starting point, but we understand that every project is unique. We're happy to customize any package to meet your specific requirements. Simply contact us to discuss your needs, and we'll provide a tailored quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers. For larger orders, we can also set up net 30 terms for qualified businesses. A 50% deposit is required for all custom orders, with the balance due upon completion.

Are there additional costs for shipping?

Shipping costs are not included in our package prices and are calculated based on weight, dimensions, destination, and service level. We offer standard ground shipping, expedited shipping, and overnight delivery options. Local pickup is available at our facility free of charge during business hours.

What is your turnaround time for standard orders?

Our standard turnaround times vary by product and quantity. Business cards typically take 3-5 business days, while larger projects like banners or promotional products may take 5-7 business days. Rush services are available for an additional fee, with some products eligible for next-day or same-day service. Production time begins after proof approval.

Do you offer any guarantees on your products?

Yes, we stand behind the quality of our products with our 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with the quality of your order, we'll reprint it at no additional cost or provide a refund. Please note that this guarantee covers print quality issues, not customer errors in design or content.
