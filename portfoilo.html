Eco Café Branding

Branding

Project Overview

Eco Café needed a complete brand identity that reflected their commitment to sustainability while maintaining a premium café experience. We created a cohesive branding package including logo design, business cards, menus, packaging, and signage.

The Challenge

The client wanted a brand identity that would communicate their eco-friendly values while maintaining a modern, upscale aesthetic. The materials needed to be functional and durable while utilizing sustainable materials.

Our Solution

We developed a brand identity featuring earthy tones with vibrant green accents and a clean, modern logo. All print materials were produced using recycled papers and vegetable-based inks. The business cards, menus, and packaging incorporate subtle texture to convey natural elements.

Deliverables

  • Logo Design
  • Business Cards
  • Menus
  • Packaging
  • Signage
  • Brand Guidelines

Materials Used

  • 100% Recycled Paper
  • Vegetable-Based Inks
  • Kraft Paper Packaging
  • FSC-Certified Products

Results

The new branding was implemented across all customer touchpoints, creating a consistent and memorable brand experience. Eco Café reported a 30% increase in customer recognition and positive feedback on their environmentally conscious approach.
